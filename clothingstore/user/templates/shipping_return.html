Shipping & Return Policy

Everything you need to know about our delivery and return process.

Returns & Exchange Policy – Clauch

At Clauch, we ensure that every product is carefully checked before dispatch. However, if you receive a defective or damaged item, we are happy to offer an exchange under the following conditions:

Return Period

  • Customers can request an exchange within 4 days after delivery only if the product is defective or damaged.
  • Requests made after 4 days from delivery will not be accepted.

Return Conditions

  • Exchanges are accepted only if the product is defective or damaged.
  • No money refunds — only product exchange will be provided.
  • The product must be unused, unwashed, and in its original packaging with tags intact.
  • Items not meeting these conditions will not be eligible for exchange.

Shipping Costs

  • If the product is confirmed to be defective/damaged, Clauch will bear the shipping cost for the exchange.
  • In all other cases, shipping charges will be the responsibility of the customer.

Exchange Process

  1. Contact our support team within the 4-day window, providing your order details and clear photos/videos of the defective item.
  2. Once approved, you will receive return instructions.
  3. After receiving and verifying the returned product, we will dispatch your replacement.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Customised or printed orders are not eligible for return or exchange unless they arrive defective or damaged.

Shipping Policy – Clauch

At Clauch, we are committed to delivering your orders quickly, safely, and efficiently.

Delivery Time

  • Within Maharashtra: Orders are typically delivered within 3–6 business days after dispatch.
  • Outside Maharashtra (Other States): Orders are typically delivered within 6–13 business days after dispatch.

Note: Delivery times may vary for remote or rural areas, during peak seasons, or due to unforeseen courier delays.

Shipping Charges

  • Shipping charges are calculated based on the total weight of your order.
  • The applicable shipping cost will be displayed at checkout before payment.

Delivery Partner

We use Shiport as our trusted logistics partner for safe and reliable deliveries.

Order Processing Time

  • All confirmed orders are processed and dispatched within 48 hours (excluding Sundays and public holidays).

Order Tracking

As soon as your order is dispatched, you will receive a tracking ID via email or SMS to monitor your shipment in real-time.

Important Notes

  • For bulk or custom orders, shipping timelines may differ — our team will inform you in advance.
  • Please check your parcel upon delivery. If it appears damaged or tampered with, kindly refuse the delivery and contact our support team immediately.

Clauch reserves the right to update the Shipping & Return policy at any time without prior notice.
