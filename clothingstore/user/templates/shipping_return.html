Shipping & Return Policy

Everything you need to know about our delivery and return process.

Shipping Policy

  • Orders are usually processed within 1-2 business days.
  • Delivery timelines vary by location and typically take 3-7 business days within India.
  • You will receive a tracking link once your order has been dispatched.
  • We currently offer standard shipping across India. International shipping will be available soon.
  • Shipping charges (if applicable) will be displayed during checkout.

Return & Exchange Policy

  • You may request a return or exchange within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unused, unworn, and in their original packaging with all tags intact.
  • Certain items such as innerwear and clearance sale items are not eligible for returns.
  • Refunds will be processed to your original payment method within 7-10 business days after approval.
  • To initiate a return, please contact our support team with your order ID.

Clauch reserves the right to update the Shipping & Return policy at any time without prior notice.
